Abbotsford Periodontist & Dental Implants is as dental clinic dealing with gum diseases and esthetic and plastic surgeries of oral tissue.

We provide dental implant treatment to replace missing teeth with special emphasis on soft tissue esthetics. Our clinic is equipped with IV Sedation facilities.


We use a state of the art dental microscope to ensure the highest amount of accuracy and perfection while performing your treatment.

Our dental microscope ensures that your teeth will be checked and cleaned thoroughly.


 

Dr. Roy is a certified specialist in Periodontics. After acquiring his dental degree from University of British Columbia (UBC), he took three years of post graduate training in Periodontics from University of Detroit Mercy, where he received his Master’s degree.


His area of research is bone and tissue grafts with bio-engineered materials. He has a special interest in periodontal micro-surgery.

 

Preserving and developing natural tissue aesthetics in the treatment of dental implants and gum problems is his primary interest. With the use of minimal invasive techniques and clinical microscope we know outstanding results can be achieved.

 
 

Our mission is to provide comprehensive, conservative, and highest standard of periodontal and dental implant treatment with the state of the art dental technology.
