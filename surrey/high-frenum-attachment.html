High Frenum attachment



     

    The muscle between our lips and gums tissue is known as frenum. In some situation this frenum attachment is close to the gum margin which results in recession of gums, or gaps between the teeth. With minor correction of this muscle attachment the health of the gums can be preserved. This procedure, which is provided at Abbotsford Periodontist & Dental Implants, is known as Frenectomy.
